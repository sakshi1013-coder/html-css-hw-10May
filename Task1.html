What is CSS?

CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) is used to style HTML elements, controlling layout, colors, fonts, and more.

What is meaning of Markup?

Markup refers to a system of symbols or tags inserted within a text document to define its structure, formatting, or relationships between different parts of the content.
